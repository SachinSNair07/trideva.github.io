About Us

Crafted From the
Heart of Kerala

Trideva Interactive is an indie game studio led by a solo BCA student developer, building games rooted in Indian culture, real-world environments, and authentic storytelling. From Kerala's bustling city roads to its spine-chilling folklore — we craft experiences that resonate.

Roadmap

Development Timeline

From concept to launch — the full journey ahead.

2024 – 2025 · Completed
Concept & Prototyping

World design, bus physics prototype, and early route mapping for TVM Bus Simulator. Core gameplay loops established.

Q1–Q2 2026 · In Progress
TVM Bus Simulator — Beta

Full route implementation, NPC passenger system, weather engine, and polishing for May 2026 launch on PC & Mobile.

12 MAY 2026 · Launch
TVM Bus Simulator — Launch 🚀

Official release on PC & Mobile platforms. Vizhinjam to East Fort route live with full NPC, weather & conductor systems.

Oye Chor

Development begins post-TVM launch. Endless runner with Punjabi culture, police chase mechanics, and online leaderboards.

2027
Yakshiyugam
